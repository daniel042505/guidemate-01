About GuideMate

Your trusted local trip booking platform in Cebu

GuideMate helps travelers discover local destinations, connect with verified tour guides, and book island and city experiences in one place.

Explore Experiences

Our Mission

Make local travel easier, safer, and more meaningful by pairing tourists with reliable guides and clear trip details before every booking.

What We Offer

  • Local spots and destination recommendations
  • Guide booking with status updates
  • Trip planning support for groups and solo travelers

Why GuideMate

We focus on transparent listings, easy booking flow, and practical tools so users can spend less time searching and more time enjoying Cebu.

Built for travelers and local communities

GuideMate is designed as a student-built tourism platform that supports both visitors and local guides. We aim to promote Cebu experiences while making bookings simple and organized.
